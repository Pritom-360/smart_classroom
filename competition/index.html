Research Visionary Challenge 2026 - Competition Hub

Empowering university students, researchers, and educators through interactive science & tech challenges.

About the Challenge

The Research Visionary Challenge 2026 is hosted by Smart Classroom to foster research interest, coding prowess, and technical innovation among university students, teachers, and professional researchers in Bangladesh. Registration is completely free and supports both single entrants and collaborative teams.

Academic Segments & Topics

Participants can register and submit projects in multiple academic and non-academic categories:

  • Computer Science & Programming: Algorithmic research, structuring, and machine learning simulations.
  • Physics & Mathematics: Interactive simulator design, gravity waves, electromagnetism, and calculus-based modeling.
  • General Science & Innovation: Educational solutions aimed at improving digital classrooms across the nation.

Certificate & Participant Badge Eligibility

Every registered candidate who submits their project files before the deadline receives a verified digital certificate. Participants also obtain a downloadable, customized profile badge that can be shared across social channels like LinkedIn and Facebook to demonstrate their research contributions.
